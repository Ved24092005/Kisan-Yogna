INFORMATION:-


PM Kisan Yojana, or Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana,
is an initiative launched by the Government of India to provide financial assistance to farmers across the country.
The scheme aims to support small and marginal farmers by offering direct income support of ₹6,000 annually,
which is disbursed in three equal installments of ₹2,000 each. This financial aid helps farmers with various
agricultural activities such as purchasing seeds, fertilizers, and other necessary inputs.
Launched in December 2018, the scheme has been a vital step in addressing the financial challenges faced
by farmers, especially those who do not have regular access to credit.
The scheme is directly transferred to the bank accounts of the beneficiaries,
ensuring transparency and reducing the chances of corruption.
It plays a significant role in improving farmers' livelihoods and contributing to the overall
growth of the agricultural sector in India.
